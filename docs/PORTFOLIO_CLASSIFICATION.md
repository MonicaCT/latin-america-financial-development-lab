## Positioning

This repository is positioned as a doctoral-style applied economics research
project. Its strongest portfolio signal is the translation of country-specific
financial data workflows into a reproducible research pipeline with transparent
data diagnostics, visual storytelling, and policy-oriented interpretation.

## Quality Standard

The project should be evaluated as a research engineering artifact. Its quality
depends on transparent data provenance, reproducible scripts, clear variable
construction, honest missing-data reporting, editorial visualization, documented
econometric assumptions, and dashboard usability for non-technical decision
makers.
